MD. MONTASIR AMIN RISHAD

M.A. Rishad acronym of Md. Montasir Amin Rishad is a Bangladeshi citizen who has a lot of interest in Web and Cyber field.
Rishad was in Mymensingh and finished his school life in Mymensingh Zilla school, one of the famous school in Bangladesh.Then he got admition into Ananda Mohan College,a famous govt. school of Bangladesh.After completing his college life he took admission in C.S.E. department under Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University, a public university also located in Mymensingh.
